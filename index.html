I am a DPhil student in Astrophysics at the University of Oxford working on new receivers for millimeter-wave astronomy. These receivers will help us to understand star formation: both in our galaxy and across cosmic time. This is crucial for understanding galaxy formation, evolution and dynamics; stellar evolution; and cosmology.

The receivers that I work on are based on Superconductor/Insulator/Superconductor junctions. These receivers use quantum tunnelling to achieve mixing sensitivity close to the quantum limit.
