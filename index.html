I am a Submillimeter Array (SMA) Fellow at the Harvard & Smithsonian Center for Astrophysics . My research is focused on advanced submillimeter-wave receivers for radio astronomy. Specifically, I am working to develop new focal plane arrays using SIS mixers with wide IF bandwidth. Among other applications, these receivers help us to study star formation, both in our galaxy and across cosmic time. SIS receivers were also recently used to image the event horizon of a black hole.
